Our Product

AI-driven tools designed to assist clinicians in the early detection and diagnosis of Alzheimer’s disease.

Why AlzhAI?

Current methods for diagnosing Alzheimer’s are often slow, complex, and rely on late-stage symptoms. At AlzhAI, we aim to transform this process by providing clinicians with AI-powered decision support tools that are accurate, transparent, and clinically meaningful.

Key Features

Early Detection

Deep learning models trained to identify early signs of Alzheimer’s from clinical data and MRI & PET imaging.

Explainable AI

Transparent predictions powered by interpretable AI, helping clinicians trust and understand results.

Predictive Insights

Risk stratification and progression forecasts to support personalized treatment strategies.

Clinician-Centric

Designed to integrate into clinical workflows, supporting — not replacing — medical expertise.
